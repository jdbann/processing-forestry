Forestry

200 Response

Welcome to Forestry! You are Head of Operations for a small patch of forest land. Your employees (lumberjacks) are eagerly awaiting instructions.

However, they can only talk to you by making HTTP requests. So your first job is to write a server.

“I’m ready for work!”

When a new lumberjack is ready to start work, they make an announcement. It looks like this:

POST /people
Host: localhost
Port: 4567
Content-Type: application/x-www-form-urlencoded

id=24f2caed-0fe4-48e6-adfc-a2ea2cd730cd&x=8&y=11

They provide you with their employee id and their current location as a form encoded string.

Taking Sinatra as an example, those details are already made available as your params object:

params = {
  "id" => "24f2caed-0fe4-48e6-adfc-a2ea2cd730cd",
  "x" => "8",
  "y" => "11"
}

How to succeed

To complete this first challenge, you just need to give a 200 response for this request.
